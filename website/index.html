About Agniphy

Agniphy is an educational platform dedicated to simplifying the world of physics for students across India. We bring together interactive lessons, board-wise study materials, and video-based learning to make physics engaging and accessible.

Our Vision

To ignite curiosity and empower learners to explore the universe through physics.

Our Mission

We aim to provide comprehensive and visually engaging physics content aligned with major education boards, helping students learn smarter and deeper.

"The beauty of physics lies in understanding the universe — and at Agniphy, we make that journey simple and fascinating."
