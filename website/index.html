About Us

Agniphy is an educational platform dedicated to simplifying complex concepts in physics. Our mission is to make learning interactive, accessible, and exciting for every student. Whether you're preparing for board exams or exploring advanced topics, Agniphy guides you step by step with structured study materials, visual explanations, and engaging resources.

Our Vision & Mission

At Agniphy, we believe knowledge is not memorized — it’s experienced. Our vision is to ignite curiosity and make every learner fall in love with physics. We aim to build a community of passionate students who can think critically, explore deeply, and innovate fearlessly.
